Delivering Excellence Since 2010

AJ Swift Courier was founded with a simple mission: to make package delivery fast, reliable, and accessible to everyone. What started as a small local delivery service has grown into a global logistics powerhouse.

Today, we pride ourselves on our extensive network that spans over 200 countries, our fleet of over 1,000 vehicles, and our team of dedicated professionals who ensure every package arrives safely and on time.

Our commitment to innovation has led us to develop cutting-edge tracking technology, eco-friendly delivery solutions, and customer-centric services that set us apart in the industry.
